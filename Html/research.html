Google Photos

Visit Site

From the google photos website I took the style that they are using in their links and buttons and implemented in my website. I used their blue color in my site. Google have their own design language that they call 'Material Design'. Generally, The metaphor of material defines the relationship between space and motion. The idea is that the technology is inspired by paper and ink and is utilized to facilitate creativity and innovation. Surfaces and edges provide familiar visual cues that allow users to quickly understand the technology beyond the physical world. Elements and components such as grids, typography, color, and imagery are not only visually pleasing, but also create a sense of hierarchy, meaning, and focus. Emphasis on different actions and components create a visual guide for users.
